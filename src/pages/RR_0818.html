The Research Revealed newsletter brings you behind the scenes so you can make the most of the vast research operations support network at BU. Please submit your newsletter ideas, and visit the Research Support website for more information.

Sponsored Programs and the Office of Research are now accepting nominations for this year’s Excellence in Research Administration award, which honors two staff members — one in central administration and the other at a school, college, or center/institute — for exceptional service to the university.

The Nanotechnology Innovation Center has awarded seed funding to projects led by Chen Yang, Xue Han, Xin Zhang, and Stephan Anderson.

The Pardee Center for the Study of the Longer Range Future published a conference report stemming from its Symposium on Global Health and the Social Sciences.

Two new Affinity Research Collaboratives — one on tobacco regulatory science and the other on fibrosis in pathology — have been established to promote team science.

The BUSM Metabolic Phenotyping and IVIS Imaging Core is conducting a survey on faculty members’ needs for mouse vascular or cardiovascular phenotyping.

Applications for the Social Innovation on Drug Resistance (SIDR) Postdoctoral Program are due on Sept. 28.

Pre-proposals for the Johnson & Johnson Innovation Lung Cancer Center pilot projects are due on Sept. 7.

Researchers from BU’s Center for Information & Systems Engineering won a $900k National Science Foundation grant for predictive analytics and personalized management of heart disease and diabetes.

The Initiative on Cities released Leading the American City, a five-part video history of mayoral leadership in the U.S.

The Danielsen Institute has been awarded the Templeton Flourishing Grant for clinical and training research related to mental health care.

Shereen Shermak has been named the new executive director of the Hariri Institute-affiliated Boston Women’s Workforce Council.

New per diem animal rates have been announced.

Registrations for the 2018 a2ru conference are now open.

The BU Office of Research is seeking faculty input on what types of topics would be most helpful for university researchers to explore in the Strategic Communications Series.

PAFO has created a new onboarding pack for departments.

Nominations for the BU Innovator of the Year Award are due on Sept. 14.
Research Open House
Sept. 1, Kilachand Center for Integrated Life Sciences & Engineering

For faculty seeking grants to support their research, writing successful proposals and navigating the process from submission to closeout is a demanding and time consuming process. This open house will address the essentials of securing and managing funding while at BU and will allow you to meet representatives from the offices that will support your research endeavors. Faculty and staff from any discipline are welcome to attend!
